# weekly-review-automation

status: seed

**Pitch:** parts of the weekly review are mechanical — glance at STATUS.md, skim the
last receipts, check for stale flags. A small script, or a scheduled notify-only report,
could pre-chew that and leave only the deciding to the human.

**Why it might earn its place:** the review happens more often when starting it costs
nothing.

**Why it might not:** the manual glance is already fast, and reading a report about the
repo is not the same as looking at the repo.

**To reach drafting:** list which review steps are actually mechanical, and which only
look mechanical.
